Frequently Asked Questions

What subjects do you offer courses in?

We offer courses in a wide range of subjects including mathematics, science, language arts, history, and more.

Are your courses suitable for all age groups?

Yes, our courses are designed to cater to learners of all ages, from elementary school students to adult learners.

Do you offer live classes or are they pre-recorded?

Our courses feature a combination of pre-recorded video lessons, interactive quizzes, and live sessions with instructors for a comprehensive learning experience.

How can I track my progress in a course and how to track anothers?

Each course includes progress tracking tools such as quizzes, assignments, and assessments to help you monitor your learning journey.

Are your courses accredited?

While our courses may not be accredited, they are designed by experienced educators and aligned with national curriculum standards to ensure quality education.

Can I access the courses on mobile devices?

Yes, our platform is mobile-friendly, allowing you to access course materials and participate in lessons from your smartphone or tablet.

Do you offer any financial aid or scholarships if I buy more then one?

We offer various financial aid options and scholarships to eligible students. Please visit our financial aid page for more information.

How long do I have access to a course after purchase?

Once you enroll in a course, you have unlimited access to the course materials for the lifetime of the course.

Are there any prerequisites for enrolling in a course?

Prerequisites vary depending on the course. You can find detailed information about course prerequisites on the course description page.

Do you offer certificates upon course completion?

Yes, we offer certificates of completion for most of our courses. These certificates can be downloaded and shared upon successfully finishing a course.
